Executive board application:

  1. Below is a list of the positions and the duties involved, choose a position (or positions) that best suits you.
  2. Download and fill out the Application form (below)
  3. Send the application attached in an email to eboard@gh2hg.org

Vice President

  • Help the president in doing his job, however the president needs
  • Complete and accomplish important tasks assigned to them
  • Follow up with jobs and tasks assigned to other members
  • Attend the inner meetings with the President and Secretary

Secretary

  • Represent the organization in the student life office
  • Issues accomplishments / semester report to be viewed by the board of trustees and the public
  • Document and save
    • All paperwork associated with the organization
    • Meeting agendas
    • All the Money owned by the organization and the transactions

Medical supplies coordinator

  • Publish the inventory for each shipment
  • Follow up with the warehouse coordinator about the medical supplies in the big warehouse

Warehouse keeper

  • Make sure everything is in place and organized in the warehouse
  • Make sure there is enough supplies (like medical supplies, tape, boxes..etc) for the volunteers to use
  • Be present in the warehouse and know when shipments will come or leave

Social media manager

  • Updates the accounts of the organizations on the social media networks.
  • Takes a lot of pictures of any events, or activities held by GH2HG and upload the pictures to the social media sites
  • Accomplish any extra assigned tasks

Volunteer coordinator

  • Keeps track of volunteers
  • Recruit new volunteers
  • Issues the biweekly time sheet reports
